We have to make thousands of decisions everyday. This was one of the homework activity that challenged us to consider a implementing a chance mechanism to make decisions for us in a day. I decided to come up with a "Yes, No, Maybe" generator that I would use to influence my decisions.

This activity made me think of a fortune cookie. Although most of us would probably not take the fortunes very seriously, once in a while it sure is fun to take out rationality and leaves things to chance. Therefore a fortune cookie animation!
